# Hide 'n' Seek!

A small pixelated browser game where you play as Tim and find hidden people across a sketch-map world.

Open `index.html` in a browser to play.

Controls:

- Use the main menu buttons for START, SETTINGS, and HOW TO PLAY.
- SETTINGS has a Cheats option. YES means Tim cannot get hurt; NO means Tim can get hurt.
- Click an unlocked place on the map to start that place's mission.
- Arrow keys or WASD to move.
- Click or tap the map to walk toward that spot.
- Press F5 to switch between Map View and First Person.
- In First Person, click doors or press Space to interact.
- On mobile, the on-screen top arrow can climb trees and other climbable things.
- Found people follow Tim around.
- Finding Mrs Grove wins instantly.
- The map has transparent locks. Snow starts open, then missions unlock The Runes, Desert, Forest, Lava Lands, and The Ruins.
- After The Ruins maze is complete, Tim is teleported to a different map.
- From Level 2 onward, Maya, Nora, Glynis, Daniel A, Daniel G, Nola, and Bob join the seek list.

The map includes Snow, Baskerville, The Runes, Desert, Forest, The Ruins, and Lava Lands.

The different map includes Cheese Land, Bridge Town, Rainforest, Lightning City, and Scarve City.

Forest mission: find Lorenco, Max, Daniel A, and Connor.

Region dangers:

- Forest has snipers.
- Desert has sandstorms that push Tim back.
- Lava Lands has lava pools that are instant death.
- Baskerville has angry humans that chase Tim.
- Snow slows Tim down and slowly drains hearts.
- The Runes has dragons that breathe fire for two-heart damage.
- The Ruins has magic keys, then a question-mark maze that gives Tim zapping and slashing super powers. Press F to zap and K to slash.
- Cheese Land has poisonous cheese that turns Tim green and slowly hurts him.
- Bridge Town has friendly phone people who ignore Tim, plus holes that send Tim to Heaven.
- Rainforest has bears that chase Tim and remove one heart.
- Lightning City has rain and lightning that removes two hearts.
- Scarve City has golfers shooting golf balls that remove one heart.

When Tim dies, he goes to Heaven. Heaven has other humans, a coin shop for in-game coins, an item shop with Choc, AI companions, and Revivers, plus a GO OUT OF HEAVEN button to come out.

The top-left Remove all abilites button clears AI companions, Choc speed, super powers, and Revivers.
